The SOAR Score

SOAR stands for Strength of Authentic Relationships.

A way to understand how meaningfully you can connect with the people in your network.

Most networks show you who you know. SOAR shows you who you can actually reach. Every contact has a connection strength score, based on real communication and trust signals like email frequency, message history, and recency of interaction. It’s the difference between a contact and a connection that matters.

🔒

Your Smart Address Book is private by default, only you see your SOAR Scores and contact profiles.

Integrates with Gmail, Outlook, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp.

How it works

Ask smarter, not louder.

One precise ask to the right person beats a broadcast to five hundred. SOAR makes the right person obvious.

01

Type your ask in plain language

No keywords to fill in, no forms to complete. Write what you need. SOAR extracts the signals, keywords, domain, context, automatically.

02

See who in your network will actually help

SOAR surfaces contacts ranked by SOAR Score and keyword density, not follower counts, not job titles. The people most likely to respond, first.

03

Send, track, and rank the outcome

Send via email or WhatsApp. Track in your inbox. Every completed ask feeds back into the engine, your next ask is always smarter than your last.
